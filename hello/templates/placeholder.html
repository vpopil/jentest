Modern applications rely heavily on OSS packages. So how do you find good ones? Search engines only know what you tell them, and humans can't keep track of the millions of OSS packages available.

Our approach is to instead look at the dependencies in your application. We feed these to a machine learning algorithm, which recommends packages that are tailored to your specific application.

What do we look for in a package?

Lasting

Good packages aren't just popular today, but tomorrow as well.

Compatible

Good packages play nicely with the packages you already use.

Unique

Good packages don't duplicate functionality in other packages.

Reused

Good packages are often the ones your company already uses.

Exceptional

Good packages are the needles in a haystack of millions of packages.
